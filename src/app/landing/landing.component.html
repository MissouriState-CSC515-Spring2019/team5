Welcome to our Landing Page

This site is fun proof of concept for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition character creation. The function is to provide a basis for quickly making a character and gettting some of the basic information to flesh that out.

Simply choose a class and a race and you will be provided a basic overview of what modifiers would be on a class of your chosen race. It also provides a quick overview of the race and abilities that provides to your character.

For further information on creating a character visit any of the following:

  • Official D&D site
  • Online Character Sheet
  • 5e Tools for Players and Dungeon Masters
